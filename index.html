Mapping is sort of a procedure about knowledge. Early maps actually bring the cognition of the nature territory for us human, as the artificial device for information containing and circulating. The procedure is also about finding and possessing. So in cause-effect logic, map is the result of recording the physical/tangible findings, but later on also, works as the device that enables people to dominate/politicalize/control/manipulate/improve/change/maintain the actual land they stand on.
